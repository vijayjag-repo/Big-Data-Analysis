The SEC finished the tournament 12-7.Of course, the Big Ten won the conference battle this year in another, important respect: the money. The league has earned at least $35.5 million with its showing this year, based on the payout system the NCAA uses to distribute money from the massive television deal for March Madness. The ACC made at least $33.8 million and the SEC with its highest share ever generated at least $32.1 million.___noonThe last game of the long grind of the NCAA Tournament is finally here: Texas Tech, Virginia, a championship on the line.It's setting up as a clinic on defense.
